## Material Stock Report

**Location:** Reports > Material Stock

**Purpose:** Get a real-time snapshot of current inventory levels for all materials across warehouses.

### Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Comprehensive Stock Information">
    - Current stock quantity and value
    - Initial stock levels
    - Stock valuation (current stock price)
    - Total weight calculations
    - Multi-warehouse support
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Advanced Filtering Options">
    - Filter by warehouse (single or multiple)
    - Search by barcode, name, mnemonic code, specifications, or model
    - Filter by material category
    - Filter by storage location/shelf position
    - Toggle zero-stock items (show/hide)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Detailed Material Attributes">
    - Product images with zoom capability
    - Barcode, name, specifications, model
    - Color, brand, manufacturer
    - Category and storage position
    - Unit and cost price information
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### How to Use

1. **Access the Report**
   - Navigate to **Reports > Material Stock**
   - The report loads with current stock data for all warehouses

2. **Filter Your Data**
   ```
   Warehouse: Select one or more warehouses
   Material Info: Enter barcode, name, or other identifiers
   Category: Choose from material category tree
   Storage Position: Filter by warehouse location
   Zero Stock: Choose to show or hide items with zero inventory
   ```

3. **View Stock Details**
   - Click **"Flow"** (流水) to view material transaction history
   - Click **"Distribution"** (分布) to see stock across warehouses
   - View summary totals at the top: Total Stock, Total Stock Value, Total Weight

4. **Export and Print**
   - Click **Export** to download as Excel file
   - Click **Print** to generate a printable report

<Note>
  The report displays summary statistics at the top showing total inventory quantity, total inventory value, and total weight across all filtered items.
</Note>

## In/Out Stock Report

**Location:** Reports > In/Out Stock Report

**Purpose:** Analyze inventory movements over a specific period, showing opening balance, receipts, issues, and closing balance.

### Key Metrics

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Period Analysis">
    - **Previous Period Balance** (上期结存): Quantity at start of period
    - **Inbound Quantity** (入库数量): Total receipts during period
    - **Outbound Quantity** (出库数量): Total issues during period
    - **Current Period Balance** (本期结存): Quantity at end of period
    - **Closing Stock Value** (结存金额): Value of ending inventory
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Filtering Capabilities">
    - Date range selection (stock period)
    - Multi-warehouse selection
    - Material search (barcode, name, specifications)
    - Category filtering
    - Material property filtering
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### How to Use

1. **Select Stock Period**
   - Choose start and end dates for analysis
   - Default shows previous month to current date

2. **Apply Filters**
   ```
   Stock Period: Select date range (required)
   Warehouse: Choose warehouses to include
   Material Info: Search specific materials
   Category: Filter by material category
   ```

3. **Analyze Stock Movements**
   - Review opening balance for each item
   - See total inbound and outbound quantities
   - Verify closing balance calculations
   - Check valuation of closing stock
   - View **Period Total Stock** and **Total Stock Value** at top

4. **View Distribution Details**
   - Click **"Distribution"** to see warehouse-level breakdown
   - View how stock is spread across locations

<Note>
  The closing balance calculation is automatic: Previous Balance + Inbound - Outbound = Current Balance. This helps verify inventory accuracy.
</Note>

## Stock Warning Report

**Location:** Reports > Stock Warning Report

**Purpose:** Identify materials that have exceeded maximum safe stock levels or fallen below minimum safe stock levels, with suggested action quantities.

### Warning Indicators

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Safety Stock Levels">
    - **Low Safe Stock** (最低安全库存): Minimum quantity before reorder
    - **High Safe Stock** (最高安全库存): Maximum quantity to maintain
    - **Current Stock** (库存): Present quantity on hand
    - **Suggested Inbound** (建议入库量): Recommended purchase quantity
    - **Suggested Outbound** (建议出库量): Recommended sales/transfer quantity
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Alert Criteria">
    Materials appear in this report when:
    - Current stock < Low Safe Stock (understocked)
    - Current stock > High Safe Stock (overstocked)
    
    The system calculates suggested actions to bring stock back to safe levels.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### How to Use

1. **Access Warning Report**
   - Navigate to **Reports > Stock Warning Report**
   - Report automatically shows all materials outside safe stock ranges

2. **Filter Warnings**
   ```
   Warehouse: Select specific warehouse
   Material Info: Search by barcode, name, specifications
   Category: Filter by material category
   ```

3. **Review Warnings**
   - Items below minimum show positive "Suggested Inbound"
   - Items above maximum show positive "Suggested Outbound"
   - Review safety stock thresholds for each item

4. **Take Action**
   - Create purchase orders for understocked items
   - Plan sales promotions for overstocked items
   - Adjust safety stock levels if needed
   - Export list for purchasing or warehouse team

<Warning>
  Stock warnings are only generated for materials that have safety stock levels configured in the material master data. Ensure you set appropriate minimum and maximum levels for critical items.
</Warning>

## Stock Distribution

**Location:** Available from Material Stock Report and In/Out Stock Report

**Purpose:** View how a specific material's inventory is distributed across multiple warehouse locations.

### What It Shows

- Stock quantity at each warehouse location
- Storage position/shelf location within warehouses
- Low and high safety stock levels per warehouse
- Warehouse-specific stock valuations

### How to Access

1. From **Material Stock Report** or **In/Out Stock Report**
2. Click **"Distribution"** (分布) button for any material
3. View popup with warehouse-level breakdown

### Use Cases

- **Transfer Planning**: Identify which warehouses have excess stock for transfers
- **Order Fulfillment**: Check which location has stock available for customer orders
- **Balanced Inventory**: Ensure stock is appropriately distributed across locations
- **Regional Analysis**: Understand inventory positioning by geographic region

## Report Actions

All inventory reports support these standard actions:

### Export to Excel

- Click the **Export** button (download icon)
- Downloads complete dataset as Excel file
- Includes all columns and summary totals
- File format: `.xls`

### Print Report

- Click the **Print** button (printer icon)
- Generates print-optimized layout
- Includes all filtered data and totals
- Browser print dialog opens automatically

### Column Customization

- Click the **Settings** icon (gear) in column header
- Check/uncheck columns to show or hide
- Rearrange columns by dragging
- Click **Restore Default** to reset

<Note>
  Column preferences are saved per user and persist across sessions for each report.
</Note>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Regular Monitoring Schedule">
    - Review Material Stock Report daily for critical items
    - Run In/Out Stock Report weekly for movement analysis
    - Check Stock Warning Report daily before creating purchase orders
    - Generate monthly reports for financial closing
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Configure Safety Stock Levels">
    - Set minimum and maximum stock levels for all active materials

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Stock quantities don't match expectations">
    **Solutions:**
    - Check if all transactions have been posted and approved
    - Review transaction history using the "Flow" button
    - Verify date range filters in In/Out Stock Report
    - Check if initial stock was properly set up
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="No data showing in reports">
    **Solutions:**
    - Verify you have permission to view the selected warehouses
    - Check if materials are assigned to the selected categories
    - Clear filters and search criteria
    - Ensure transactions have been posted to inventory
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Stock warning report is empty">
    **Solutions:**
    - Verify safety stock levels are configured in material master
    - Check that warehouse field is populated for materials
    - Confirm materials have current stock balances
    - Review if all items are within safe stock ranges
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Export or print not working">
    **Solutions:**
    - Check browser popup blocker settings
    - Verify browser has permission to download files
    - Try a different browser
    - Reduce dataset size by applying filters
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
